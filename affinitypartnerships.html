Here’s how Affinity Partnerships can help your business do more.

To succeed in today’s economy, companies need to offer more in the way of services. Customers aren’t just expecting it. They’re demanding it. Affinity Partnerships specializes in bringing financial services to companies who want to offer loan assistance to their members and customers. We bridge the gap through a seamless interface, which connects borrowers to lenders. Our solution is customizable and can be integrated into an existing web presence or as a stand-alone website. Our turnkey solution is already interfaced with many of the nation’s top lenders. As one of our affinity partners, your company can extend financial services to your customers.
